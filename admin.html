How to Use
1
Generate
Enter the business name, select the type and click Generate. Each key is unique and cryptographically tied to your secret.
2
Save & Send
Click "Save to List" to keep a record, then copy the key and send it to your client via email or WhatsApp.
3
Client Activates
The client opens FinanceOS, enters the key once on the license screen and it activates permanently on their device.
⚠ Keep this file private — it contains your master key algorithm. The CSV export is your backup record of all issued licenses.
License Transfer: If a client changes computer, they will see a "Device Not Authorized" screen showing their new Device ID. Generate a new key for them and they can re-activate. Old key remains blocked on old device (new localStorage = new blank state anyway).
